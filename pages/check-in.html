Arriving at the Cabin

  1. Head south on I-17 from Flagstaff, take the Kachina Village / Mountainaire exit
  2. Follow Kachina Trail to Kona Trail
  3. Look for 2634 Kona Trail — Unit B is the smaller cabin on the right
  4. Park in the driveway (pull forward to leave room)
  5. Walk up the stone steps to the front door
  6. Enter your 4-digit code on the keypad

Check-out Checklist

  • Turn off all lights and the TV
  • Set thermostat to 68°F
  • Replace the hot tub cover
  • Load dishwasher and start it
  • Gather used towels and leave on bathroom floor
  • Take trash to outdoor bin
  • Lock the door (it locks automatically)
No need to strip the beds
We'll take care of the linens. Just leave used towels on the bathroom floor and we're good!
