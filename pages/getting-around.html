Gas Stations

The nearest gas stations are in Flagstaff, about 15 minutes north. Fill up before heading to the cabin, especially in winter.

Ride Share

Uber and Lyft operate in Flagstaff but availability can be limited, especially late at night. Plan ahead if you're going out to dinner or breweries downtown.
