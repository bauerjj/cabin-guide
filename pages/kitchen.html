The Kitchen

Our kitchen has everything you need for a great meal — from a quick breakfast to a full dinner.

Appliances

  • Gas range & oven — 5-burner stove with oven
  • Full-size refrigerator with freezer
  • Coffee station — Drip coffee maker + Keurig. Coffee and filters provided.
  • Toaster
  • Microwave (if available)

What's Stocked

We provide the basics to get you started:

  • Coffee, tea, and filters
  • Cooking oil and basic spices
  • Salt & pepper
  • Dish soap and sponge
  • Paper towels
  • Trash bags

Cookware & Utensils

You'll find a full set of:

  • Pots and pans
  • Baking sheets
  • Knife set
  • Cutting board
  • Cooking utensils (spatula, wooden spoons, tongs, etc.)
  • Mixing bowls
  • Can opener, bottle opener, corkscrew

Dining

The round dining table seats 4 with comfortable leather chairs — perfect for meals or board games.

🛒 Nearest Grocery Stores
Stock up in Flagstaff before heading to the cabin (~15 min north). Options include Fry's, Safeway, Sprouts, Natural Grocers, and Whole Foods.

Before You Leave

  • Load the dishwasher and start it (if there is one) or hand-wash dishes
  • Wipe down counters
  • Take out any perishable food
