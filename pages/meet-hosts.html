About Us

We fell in love with Flagstaff's mountain charm and wanted to create a space where guests can experience it too. Our cabin has been thoughtfully renovated with modern amenities while keeping the cozy, rustic character that makes it special.

Getting in Touch

The best way to reach us is through the Airbnb app. We monitor messages closely and typically respond within an hour.

We're just a message away

Leave a Review
