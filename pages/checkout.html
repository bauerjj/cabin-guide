Before You Leave

  • Kitchen: Load and start the dishwasher, wipe counters, dispose of perishable food
  • Trash: Take all trash to the outdoor bin (keep lid closed for wildlife)
  • Towels: Leave used towels on the bathroom floor
  • Hot tub: Replace the cover securely
  • Thermostat: Set mini-split to 68°F
  • Lights: Turn off all lights
  • TV: Turn off and sign out of streaming accounts
  • Windows: Close and lock all windows
  • Door: Make sure the front door closes behind you (it locks automatically)
  • Belongings: Check the loft, bathroom, and under the couch cushions!
You don't need to...
  • Strip the beds
  • Wash the linens
  • Vacuum or sweep
  • Do a deep clean
We'll take care of all of that!

Forgot Something?

If you left something behind, message us through Airbnb right away. We'll do our best to locate and return it.

Leave a Review

We'd love to hear about your stay! Reviews help us improve and help future guests find our cabin. You can leave a review through the Airbnb app after checkout.

Thank you for staying with us! Safe travels! 🌲
