Seasonal Highlights

Winter (December - March)

  • Skiing & snowboarding at Arizona Snowbowl
  • Cross-country skiing in the Coconino National Forest
  • Snow play at Wing Mountain or Crowley Pit

Summer (June - September)

  • Hiking — hundreds of trails from easy to strenuous
  • Mountain biking on world-class trail systems
  • Kayaking/fishing at Lake Mary (~20 min)

Fall (September - November)

  • Aspen viewing — drive the Inner Basin Trail or Snowbowl road
  • Flagstaff Festival of Science (late September)
  • Cooler hiking with fewer crowds

Spring (March - May)

  • Wildflower blooms in lower elevations
  • Late-season skiing some years into April
  • Farmers markets starting up
